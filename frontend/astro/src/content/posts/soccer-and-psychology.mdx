**Key Psychological Skills in Soccer:**
- **Concentration:** Staying focused during high-pressure moments.
- **Confidence:** Believing in one's abilities, even after setbacks.
- **Team Cohesion:** Building trust and communication among teammates.
- **Resilience:** Bouncing back from mistakes or losses.
