Technology has been the driving force behind the progress of human civilization. From the invention of the wheel and stone tools to the rise of the internet and artificial intelligence, each leap has transformed how we live, work, and interact. 

The earliest technologies were simple tools for hunting and gathering. As societies advanced, so did their inventions: agriculture, writing, the printing press, and the steam engine all marked major turning points. The 20th century saw the birth of computers, the internet, and mobile devices, connecting the world like never before.

Today, technology is evolving at an unprecedented pace. Artificial intelligence, quantum computing, and biotechnology are opening new frontiers. While these advances bring incredible opportunities, they also raise ethical questions and challenges about privacy, security, and the future of work.
